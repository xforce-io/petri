# Rebut

Counter the proposer's opening argument.

1. Read the proposer's opening argument from the available artifacts (look for argument.md in the opening stage)
2. Write your rebuttal to `argument.md` (400-600 words):
   - Identify and quote the proposer's key claims
   - Challenge each with counterevidence or logical flaws
   - Present your strongest counter-argument
   - Offer an alternative perspective
3. Write gate artifact `argument.json`:
   ```json
   {"submitted": true, "word_count": N}
   ```
